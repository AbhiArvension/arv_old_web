DIGITAL SIGNAGE


Area of Expertise


Digital signage is a sub-segment of electronic signage. Digital displays use technologies such as LCD, LED, projection and e-paper to display digital images, video, web pages, weather data, restaurant menus, or text. They can be found in public spaces, transportation systems, museums, stadiums, retail stores, hotels, restaurants and corporate buildings etc., to provide wayfinding, exhibitions, marketing and outdoor advertising. They are used as a network of electronic displays that are centrally managed and individually addressable for the display of text, animated or video messages for advertising, information, entertainment and merchandising to targeted audiences.We are providing the best in the class products to our customers.

REGULAR MONITOR

These types of monitors can also be used for signage purpose. The only downside is that, they are designed for a usage of 6-hour straight, after that, they will start to overheat and will cause circuit damage. If the display should be placed inside an office and should stay on for a maximum of 8 hours, then these monitors will be the better companion among the 2.



SIGNAGE MONITOR

These types of display are the best for signage purpose as the recommended screen on time for the same is 24x7, where no overheating issues occur and the signage display will be active for its whole lifetime.
