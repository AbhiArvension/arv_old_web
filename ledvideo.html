LED VIDEO WALL



Direct view LED video wall technology is becoming increasingly popular due to its ability to deliver seamless images, scalability to any size or shape, and excellent optimal characteristics that make video wall content look great from any angle. We provide a wide range of LED displays from high-resolution indoor video walls to solutions for the biggest outdoor public and sporting venues. Featuring 24/7 operation, high-resolution visuals, modularity, low maintenance requirements and an overall low operational cost, LED-based display technologies are becoming a popular choice for video walls in a variety of settings. From architectural and digital signage displays to entertainment venues and critical viewing control room environments, video walls using LED-based displays will become the next best platform advertising products to the public. While ordering the display of your choice, keep in mind that: Starting with a pixel pitch that is as low as P1.2, depending on the viewing distance these displays can go up to P15. The lesser the viewing distance, the smaller should be the pixel pitch While selecting the size of the display, keep in mind the area of your indoor space. Large venues like convention halls or control rooms will demand a bigger size screen. Environments like control rooms need close monitoring and will require a fine pixel pitch LED video walls. On the other hand, industries like retail, where space is a concern, ultra – slim LED video walls would be the best choice. Arvension Technologies provides both indoor and outdoor LED walls. INDOOR LED VIDEO WALL



We deliver one-stop quality solutions that are tailored to the specific indoor environment. Arvension Technologies’ Indoor LED Video Walls also known as indoor LED display are built using the SMD technology (Surface Mounted Devices) to deliver content with a crystal-clear resolution. OUTDOOR LED VIDEO WALL



Arvension Technologies’ Outdoor LED Video Wall also commonly known as outdoor LED displays is an excellent medium to captivate your audience by displaying vibrant content. Outdoor LED display higher than 10mm pixel pitch are made using DIP technology making them are sturdy, durable and they have excellent picture quality even in direct sunlight.
